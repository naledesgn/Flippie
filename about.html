Our History

At Flippie, our journey began with a passion for bringing the delightful flavors of Japanese crepes and bakery treats in the heart of Over-the-Rhine, Cincinnati.

From Osaka to Over-the-Rhine

Hiro Tanaka's journey started in Osaka, Japan, where his fondest memories were filled with the aroma of his grandmother's kitchen. There, she meticulously crafted delicate Japanese crepes, a tradition that Hiro would later carry on. Inspired by his heritage and his grandmother's legacy, Hiro brought a touch of home to Cincinnati when he opened Flippie.

Nestled in the vibrant community of Over-the-Rhine, Flippie opened its doors in 2018, radiating warmth and charm from the moment guests stepped inside. Our quaint storefront quickly became a beloved gathering spot for locals and visitors alike, drawn in by the tantalizing aroma of freshly made crepes and the promise of sweet memories in the making.

As word spread of Flippie's irresistible offerings, our little bakery blossomed into a bustling hub ofactivity, bustling with laughter, conversation, and the clinking of forksagainst plates. From our signature Strawberry Dream crepe to our decadent MatchaDelight, each bite tells a story of craftsmanship and care, rooted in traditionyet infused with a modern twist.

Over the years, Flippie has become more than just a place to enjoy delicious food; it's a destination where friendships are forged, celebrations are shared, and dreams are realized. Our commitment to quality and creativity has earned us accolades from food critics and loyal patrons alike, cementing our reputation as a culinary gem in the heart of Cincinnati.

Flippie's Flourishing Future

But our story doesn't end here. As we continue to evolve and innovate, we remain dedicated to honoring our roots and staying true to the principles that have guided us from the beginning. With each new day, we look forward to welcoming you into our world, where everycrepe tells a tale and every bite is infused with the spirit of Flippie. Joinus on this delicious journey, and together, let's create memories that willlast a lifetime.
